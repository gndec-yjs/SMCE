## üìÑ 2. Structured Report Submission

**Goal:** Submit a professional, well-organized technical report on your topic.

**Structure:**
1. **Title Page** ‚Äì Title, your name, roll number, course, date
2. **Abstract** ‚Äì 150‚Äì200 words summary
3. **Table of Contents**
4. **Introduction** ‚Äì Background, objectives
5. **Main Body** ‚Äì Methodology, discussion, results, and analysis
6. **Conclusion** ‚Äì Key takeaways and recommendations
7. **References** ‚Äì As per IEEE/APA format

**Formatting:**
- Font: Times New Roman, 12 pt
- Line spacing: 1.5
- Margins: 1 inch (all sides)
- Number all figures and tables with captions

## üìë 3. Writing a Technical Article / Review Paper

**Goal:** Write a scholarly document in one of the following formats:  
- **Technical Article** ‚Äì Focused on a specific engineering problem or solution  
- **Review Paper** ‚Äì Summarizing and analyzing existing literature  
- **Bibliometric Analysis** ‚Äì Data-driven study of research trends  
- **Comprehensive Review** ‚Äì Broader scope, integrating multiple research findings  
- **Systematic Review** ‚Äì Structured method to collect and synthesize literature

**Key Points:**
- **Word Limit:** 2000‚Äì3000 words  
- Include **abstract**, **keywords**, and **references**
- Cite **minimum 10 quality sources** (journals, conference papers, books)
- Maintain **academic integrity** ‚Äì no plagiarism
- Use **professional language** and maintain technical accuracy

---


| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Technical Article** | A concise, factual write-up on a technical topic |
| **Review Paper** | Summarizes and analyzes existing literature |
| **Bibliometric Analysis** | Uses statistical tools to analyze publication trends |
| **Comprehensive Review** | Covers all aspects of a broad technical theme |
| **Systematic Review** | Follows a structured methodology to review research |

### ‚úÖ Research Writing Checklist
- [ ] Select a **focused topic**
- [ ] Collect minimum **10‚Äì15 credible references**
- [ ] Create an outline before writing
- [ ] Use appropriate **academic tone**
- [ ] Include diagrams/tables for clarity
- [ ] Ensure originality (**plagiarism < 10%**)
- [ ] Conclude with **future scope** or recommendations
